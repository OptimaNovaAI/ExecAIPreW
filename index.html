Total time 10–15 minutes Arrive with clear outcomes, one page of context, and two quick LLM outputs.

1) Set your outcomes

  • By the end of this masterclass, success looks like…
  • The three decisions my board expects from me are…
  • The biggest constraint I must respect is…

2) Gather one page of context

  • A recent internal memo or report you produce often
  • A time-consuming task that repeats weekly
  • A customer, constituent, or donor journey you want to improve

3) Run two prompts

Use-case ideas

Act as a senior strategy advisor. Given this context [paste 3–5 sentences], list five high‑ROI GenAI use cases for my organization. For each, include: problem, who benefits, time saved, data needed, and risk flags.

One-page executive brief

Turn the attached or pasted text into a one‑page executive brief. Include: KPIs, decisions needed, risks, and a 90‑day starter plan with owners.

Bring to Session 1

  • Your three outcomes from step 1
  • One page of context from step 2
  • Both prompt outputs from step 3

Privacy: do not paste confidential data into public AI tools. Use summaries or redacted text. We will cover safe workflows in class.
